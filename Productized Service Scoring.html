Decision rules
Knock-out first. Repeatable at scale and Measurable outcome must both be at least Partial. If either is No - stop. Do not productize regardless of other scores.
60% threshold on both sections. Necessary but not sufficient. A service scoring 65% with all Partials is weaker than one scoring 60% with two clear Yes answers.
At least one strong Yes in value. A service with no single criterion at full Yes has no compelling lead for the sales conversation. Identify the primary hook before productizing.
Conversation context shifts weights, not the rules. Knock-out criteria and the 60% threshold apply regardless of what is driving the deal. The context only determines which value criteria matter most.
